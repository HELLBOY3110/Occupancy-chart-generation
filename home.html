Our project is titled "Classroom Allocation & Chat Generation." It is a web-based application designed to facilitate classroom allocation and provide a chat platform for users. The project aims to streamline the process of assigning classrooms for various activities within an educational institution or organization. Overall, the project aims to simplify the process of classroom allocation, reduce manual effort, enhance communication between users, and improve the overall efficiency of managing classrooms within an educational institution or organization.
