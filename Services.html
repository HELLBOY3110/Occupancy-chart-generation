  • User Registration and Login: Users can create an account and log in to the system using their credentials. This ensures secure access to the application.
  • Classroom Allocation: The application provides a system for allocating classrooms to different activities such as lectures, seminars, workshops, or meetings. Users can request a specific classroom and specify the date, time, and purpose of the activity. The system will assign an appropriate classroom based on availability and compatibility.
  • Classroom Management: Administrators can manage and maintain information about available classrooms, including their capacity, facilities, and availability schedule. They can add, edit, and delete classroom details to ensure accurate allocation.
  • Notifications and Reminders: The system can send notifications and reminders to users regarding their allocated classrooms, upcoming activities, changes in schedules, or any relevant updates.
  • Chat Platform: The application provides a chat platform where users can communicate with each other, discuss activities, ask questions, and share information related to classrooms and allocated activities. The chat feature enhances collaboration and fosters communication among users.
  • Support and Help: Users can access support resources, FAQs, and contact administrators for assistance or guidance related to classroom allocation or any issues they encounter within the system.
